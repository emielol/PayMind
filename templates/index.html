Who We Are

PayMind's goal is to ensure that forgotten, pesky expenses do not creep up on people at random.

We want to make sure that you have knowledge further than just the price and name of the subscription, but the full details.

PayMind is your personal subscription manager. Our calendars are used keep track of your online subscriptions, and we make sure to remind you of the upcoming charge dates.

Can't afford to pay your current subscription and need an alternative? We got it; PayMind also has a machine learning feature that allows you to compare one subscription to the price of others.
